## What is Arkiv?

**Arkiv** is a decentralized data layer that makes it simple to persist, query, and share blockchain-backed datasets. Think of it as a persistent storage solution where your data is immutable, verifiable, and accessible from anywhere.

Whether you're building analytics dashboards, data-driven dApps, or need reliable blockchain data indexing, Arkiv provides the infrastructure to make it happen.
